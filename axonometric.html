Angles
Ratios

A Javascript tool for determining the side ratios of an axonometric projection, using the angles.

Left angle represents the left line measured anticlockwise from the top. Right angle is the right line measured clockwise from the top.

Either edit the angles in the boxes below, or use the diagram. Click or drag on the left side of the diagram to change the left angle, and use the right side to change the right angle.
