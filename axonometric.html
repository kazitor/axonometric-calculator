Angles
Ratios

A Javascript tool for determining the side ratios of an axonometric projection, using the angles.

Left angle represents the left line measured anticlockwise from the top. Right angle is the right line measured clockwise from the top. Both can be greater than 180°.

Either edit the angles in the boxes below, or (much more fun) use the diagram. Click left of the origin and drag to change the left angle. Start on the right to change the right angle. You can move to the other side, but only certain ranges will yield proper results.
